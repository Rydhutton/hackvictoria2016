FAQ

What is vihdi.com?

We provide hosting for video files so that videos can be stored and shared online.

Can anyone upload?

Only registered members can upload files.

Why cant I register for an account?

Currently vihdi.com is a private host. Access to the site is given through referral by another member.

Why is there no search?

As we are a private hosting site only the content that members wish to share is accessible. It is up the member to provide a link to the content.

What kind of files types are allowed?

vihdi.com deals mostly with video files. However, all files can be uploaded but not all will be accessible in the browser.

What type of content is allowed?

All content is subject to our Terms of Service. Pornography, nudity, sexual images, offensive material, and copyrighted material is all prohibited.

Where can I report a violation?

More information on reporting copyright issue's can be found on our DMCA page. All other violations can be reported through contact page.

How can I contact vihdi.com?

Visit our contact page. We would love to hear from you.
